- **Name:** Enzo

## 2. "Meet the Artist" Bio Options

_When building the "About Me" or "Meet the Artist" component, use ONE of the following text options based on the UI layout you choose. Do not invent your own bio text._

### Option A: The "Artist Trading Card" (For small, sidebar layouts)

> **Meet the Artist: Enzo**
> **Age:** 9
> **Creative Weapons of Choice:** Pencils, pens, and crayons.
> **When I'm not drawing:** You can find me gaming and building epic worlds in Roblox!
> **Favorite Place to Visit:** Japan 🇯🇵

### Option B: The Mini-Interview (For interactive or grid layouts)

> **Hello! I'm Enzo.** Welcome to my digital sketchbook!
>
> **🎨 What do I do?** > I love to draw and create new things. This website is where I keep all my favorite masterpieces.
>
> **🎮 Hobbies besides art?** > When I don't have a pencil in my hand, I am definitely playing Roblox.
>
> **✈️ Ultimate travel spot?** > Japan! It is my absolute favorite country to visit.

### Option C: The Storybook Bio (For a standard text block)

> **Welcome to Enzo's Art Studio!**
> Hi, I'm Enzo! I am a 9-year-old artist, and this is my digital sketchbook where I share all of my favorite drawings. I have a huge imagination, and when I'm not busy creating new artwork, I love hanging out and playing Roblox. My absolute favorite place in the world to visit is Japan, and you might even spot some of those fun adventures inspiring my sketches. Take a look around!

## 3. Standard UI Copy

_When creating buttons, headers, or links, use these exact phrases to maintain the playful tone:_

- **Hero Headline:** "Welcome to Enzo's Art Studio!"
- **Primary Call-to-Action Button:** "See My Drawings"
- **Gallery Header:** "My Masterpieces"
- **Lightbox Close Button (aria-label):** "Close sketchbook"
- **Footer:** "Made with 🖍️ and imagination. © [Current Year] Enzo."

## 4. Image Placeholder Directives

\*When generating the Gallery component code, assume the `public/masterpieces/` folder contains images named `sketch-1.jpg`, `sketch-2.jpg`, etc.

- ALWAYS write descriptive `alt` text for images. For placeholder code, use examples inspired by his interests, such as: `alt="A pencil sketch of a Roblox character"` or `alt="A crayon drawing of a bullet train in Japan"`.
